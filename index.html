Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • they can move their eyes separately, with each eye having a field of 180 degrees. This means they can see 360 degrees if they need to!
  • 59 unique species of chameleon live on the island of Madagascar and are found nowhere else.
  • Their tongues consist of both muscles and bones, and can bend round to grab their pray.

Interesting Facts about Cranes

  • Cranes are powerful flyers, with some having the ability to fly over the Himalayas.
  • Cranes are found on all continents except in South America and Antarctica.
  • They live in wetlands and grasslands habitats.
